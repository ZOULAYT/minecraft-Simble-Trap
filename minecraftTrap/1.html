1.Tree Trap

Material:

1 lever, 1 redstone dust, 4 any block, 4 redstone torch, 16 tnt, 24 grass block

Build:

First mine a 5 * 2 * 5 hole under a tree, then put the lever under the tree log, under the lever put the redstone dust down, put blocks around it, each with a redstone torch, put tnt around it, last cover up with grass(or any block).
